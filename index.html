RACING COACH AI

An autonomous driving AI for eSport race driver coaching

Racing Coach AI is an application that analyses your live telemetry data
and provides feedback for you to improve your driving skills.

Through the app you have access to the live comparison between
your current lap and a reference lap from a previous run.

You can improve your skills based on your previous performance,
based on the game's AI or based on your friends' performance.
Share the .rcai files generated by the app with your friends
and help them improve!
